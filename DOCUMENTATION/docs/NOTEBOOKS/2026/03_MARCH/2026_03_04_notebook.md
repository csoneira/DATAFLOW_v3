## MINGO01 STEP 4 TASK 3: charge sharing at HV-paint frontiers
---

### Observation
`MINGO01_task_3_specific_active_strips_sum2.png` shows a clear sharing of charge along the conductor at the frontiers of the HV paint. The frontier strips display broadened/split response, consistent with charge being shared between strips that are the furthest possible. If this was simple crosstalk, then it simply would be monotonical on the adjacent and then in the non adjacent, but here it is clear that there are much more counts in the 1001 case than in the 1010 and 0101 cases.
