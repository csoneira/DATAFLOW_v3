### Working on the simulation flux and expected flux in the detector
Implement I0 directly from the closed-form formula.

Given n and F (with F defined as the radian integral F = ∫_0^{π/2} I0 cos(θ)^n dθ), compute:

I0 = F * 2 * Gamma((n + 2)/2) / (sqrt(pi) * Gamma((n + 1)/2))
