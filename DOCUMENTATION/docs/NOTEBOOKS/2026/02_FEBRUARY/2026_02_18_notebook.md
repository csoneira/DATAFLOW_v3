### STEP 3 angular-efficiency update

Today I validated and integrated the new angle-dependent ionization behavior in STEP 3. The ionization mean now scales with the effective track length in the gas gap (`L/cos(theta)`), so oblique tracks naturally increase expected primary ionizations.

I also updated the angular efficiency plotting to show:
- `eff2 = 1 - N134/N1234`
- `eff3 = 1 - N124/N1234`

Both efficiencies include a Poisson-based uncertainty band and an `eff = 1` reference line.

So this might break, a priori, the agreement, adding a systematic difference.
